# Welcome Welcome to the iiPython system status page. This site tracks the status of my publicly hosted webpages to notify you of any outages and or slow ping times. Normally, my sites won't go down unless I have a severe storm or am performing maintenance. # Reliability In order to ensure that my status page is online 24/7, I decided to build this as a client-side app hosted by [Github Pages](https://pages.github.com/) rather then a full website that I host. What's the point of hosting a status page if it goes down the moment I lose power? Exactly, there isn't one. However, since this is **client-sided**, ping times will depend on **your** location, and will report my servers as down if [Cloudflare](https://cloudflare.com/) has an outage or my DNS records stop resolving for any reason. # Contact Odds are, if this page reports an outage, then I'm probably experiencing an outage. **HOWEVER**, if you feel that this page is incorrectly saying that my services are up/down when in fact they aren't, please contact me at [ben@iipython.cf](mailto:ben@iipython.cf) or on Discord at [iiPython#0768](https://discord.com/users/633185043774177280).
